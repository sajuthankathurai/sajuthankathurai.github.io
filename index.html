Already renewed? These answers may help

How do I update my billing info?

  1. Log in to your McAfee account.
  2. At the top of your My Account page, select My Account, and then click My Profile.
  3. At the top of the Billing Information section, click Update. Enter your new credit card information and billing address, and then click Save.

How do I turn off autorenewal?

  1. Go to "Auto-Renewal Settings" under My Account, click on the "Uninterrupted Protection" column and click "On".
  2. Once you confirm, we'll send an email to let you know auto-renewal has begun. Auto-renewal can be turned off at any time.
  3. To turn off auto-renewal, follow the same steps as outlined and click "Off".

How do I protect additional devices?

  1. Log in to your McAfee account.
  2. At the top of your My Account page, select My Account, and then click Subscriptions.
  3. Look for the subscription you want to add additional devices, and then click Add device. Choose the device you want to protect (PC, Mac, mobile or tablet) and either download the protection or send link to protect another device.
